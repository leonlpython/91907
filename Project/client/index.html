Welcome to the weights room!

We’re so excited to have you on board

As a valued newcomer to our fitness
family, we thought we’d send across some handy resources to make your experience as rewarding as possible.
At the weights room, we believe fitness is more than just a workout –it’s a lifestyle.
Whether you're a seasoned athlete or just starting out, our state-of-the-art facilities and supportive
community are here to help you crush your goals and feel your best. Check out our rules on the left.
If you’re keen to join, you can find all relevant information about signing up here:through the contract
or talking to a phyical ed teacher.The gyms is a safe place for everyone. There will be no discrimination.
Got questions? Simply email a physical ed teacher and they’ll get back to you soon.
We look forward to seeing you soon!

Rules

  1. Always use the weights with an adult present
  2. Use appropriate shoes and clothing
  3. Sign into logbook before using the weights room
  4. No food or drink permitted other than water
  5. Clean and return equipment after use
  6. Use machines and weights only as intended
  7. If an injury occurs inform staff immediately.
  8. Follow all normal school rules.
